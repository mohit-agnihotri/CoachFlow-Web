Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. Role of CoachFlow

CoachFlow operates solely as a technology platform connecting students with their respective tutors/academies. We facilitate the tracking and processing of fee payments but do not directly provide educational services.

2. Refund Eligibility

Any requests for cancellations or refunds of tuition fees must be directed strictly to the respective academy or tutor. CoachFlow does not hold funds and cannot issue refunds directly to students once a payment has been successfully settled to the tutor's account.

3. Dispute Resolution

If a student believes a payment was made in error or due to fraud, they must immediately contact the academy. If the academy agrees to a refund, they must initiate it manually. CoachFlow is not liable for disputes between students and tutors.
