Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use CoachFlow, we collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, and profile information. For teachers accepting payments, we collect KYC information (such as PAN and bank account details) to verify your identity and process payouts securely via Cashfree.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and provide the features of CoachFlow. This includes processing payments, providing customer support, and using AI (Gemini) to process student queries against your academy's FAQs.

3. Data Security

We implement strict security measures, including Row Level Security (RLS) on our databases and EncryptedSharedPreferences on the device, to protect your personal information. Payment processing is handled entirely by Cashfree, and we do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
